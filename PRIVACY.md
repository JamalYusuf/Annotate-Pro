# Annotate Pro Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 4, 2026

Annotate Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles information.

## 1. Overview

Annotate Pro is a **fully local** annotation tool. It allows you to draw, add shapes, use a laser pointer, and capture screenshots directly on any webpage. 

**We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.** All functionality happens entirely within your browser.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect **zero** personal information.

- We do **not** collect browsing history, page content, or any data from the websites you visit.
- We do **not** use analytics, tracking pixels, or any telemetry.
- We do **not** require accounts, logins, or any form of user identification.

## 3. How Annotations and Data Are Handled

| Type of Data              | Where It Lives                  | What Happens to It                          | Shared? |
|---------------------------|----------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|---------|
| Drawings & Annotations    | Current browser tab memory      | Exists only while the tab is open           | Never   |
| Preferences (colors, sizes, dock position, font scale) | `chrome.storage.local`     | Stored locally in your browser              | Never   |
| Screenshots               | Generated locally, then downloaded via Chrome | Saved to your Downloads folder           | Never   |

All annotations are stored as temporary `ImageData` in the current tab only. They are automatically cleared when you reload or close the page.

## 4. Permissions We Use & Why

| Permission          | Purpose                                                                 | Required? |
|---------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------|---------|
| `activeTab`         | Access the current tab when you activate annotation                      | Yes     |
| `scripting`         | Inject the annotation tools only when you explicitly turn them on        | Yes     |
| `storage`           | Save your preferences locally (dock side, colors, sizes, etc.)           | Yes     |
| `downloads`         | Allow one-click auto-download of annotated screenshots                   | Yes     |
| `<all_urls>`        | Enable annotation on **any** webpage you choose to annotate              | Yes     |

**Important:** The extension **never** runs automatically. The annotation tools are only loaded when you click the extension icon or press `Ctrl+Shift+A`.

## 5. Third-Party Services

Annotate Pro does **not** use any third-party services, analytics providers, or external APIs.

No data ever leaves your device.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do **not** share, sell, or rent any information because we do not collect any.

## 7. Your Rights & Control

You have full control at all times:

- You can clear all annotations on the current page using the **Clear All** button.
- You can reset all preferences from the **Settings** panel.
- You can uninstall the extension at any time to remove all locally stored data.
- Annotations do not persist across page reloads or browser restarts.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Annotate Pro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
